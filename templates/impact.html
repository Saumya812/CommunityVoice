🧠 Responsible AI for Sensitive Cases
✓ Mental health cases are never auto-resolved. All mental health intakes are escalated to a qualified human counselor. The AI explicitly tells users they will speak with a licensed professional — not a bot.
✓ Crisis keyword detection on every message. 988, Crisis Text Line, and 911 resources are surfaced immediately — before intake continues — when crisis language is detected.
✓ Safety notice is always visible. Community members see "This is an AI intake assistant — not a therapist" before their first message. There is no ambiguity about what the system is.
✓ Human follow-up on every case. Every case logged receives a staff review. Auto-resolution handles logistics — human judgment handles care.
